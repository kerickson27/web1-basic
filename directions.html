By Metro

  • Take the Green Line to the Navy Yard metro.
  • Exit towards New Jersey Ave.
  • Head south on New Jersey Ave SE.
  • Turn left on Tingey Street.
  • Turn right on 3rd St SE.

By Bus

Multiple Metro bus routes (A42, A46, A48, P1, P2, V7, V8, V9) have stops along M Street SE. Visit the Metrobus website for details and maps.

By Bike

Bike towards Navy Yard/Capitol Rivefront, and follow directions from the Metro above. There are bike racks available throughout the park. There is also a Capital Bikeshare conveniently located at New Jersey Ave and M Street SE.

Parking

Paid parking is available at any of the Yards' parking lots.
